Sweet Potato Gnocchi

Description

A new, yummy twist on gnocchi. You can top this sweet potato gnocchi with butter or your favorite Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet potatoes
  • salt
  • 1 egg
  • flour
  • 1 clove garlic, pressed
  • ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Bake sweet potatoes until soft to the touch.
  3. Remove skin from the potatoes and mash into a large bowl.
  4. Blend in egg, garlic, salt, and nutmeg.
  5. Mix in flour until a soft dough forms.
  6. Boil water in a pot and roll the dough on a floured surface into logs.
  7. Drop the dough pieces into the pot and boil until they float to the surface.
  8. Serve with butter or Alfredo sauce and enjoy.
