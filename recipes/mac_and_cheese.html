Mac and Cheese

Description

A homemade alternative to boxed mac and cheese that's just as quick and easy. This stovetop mac and cheese uses staple ingredients that come together in just 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • flour
  • unsalted butter
  • milk
  • shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook macaroni in boiling water for about 8 minutes.
  2. At the same time, melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in milk slowly and cook until the mixture is smooth, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add cheese and stir until melted.
  6. Add macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until fully coated.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy.
