Beef Goulash

Description

A classic Eastern European recipe, prepared with tender chunks of beef, savory vegetables, and fragrant spices. It is a family favorite comfort food, and absolutely delicious when served with egg noodles or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 pounds beef chuck roast
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • flour
  • Olive oil
  • unsalted butter
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • paprika
  • ground carraway seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef stock broth
  • egg noodles or mashed potatoes to serve alongside

Steps

  1. Place beef chunks into a large bowl, season with salt and black pepper; add 2 tablespoons of flour to coat.
  2. Place 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a pan over medium heat and brown all sides of the meat for about 2 to 3 minutes. Remove the meat from the pan and set aside
  3. Add olive oil and butter to the same pan, and add the onions and carrots; saute for about 2 minutes and add the parika, carraway seeds and bay leaves.
  4. Add garlic, and stir in the tomato paste. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of flour and stir. Then add the beef stock broth followed by the diced tomatoes.
  5. Add the beef into the pan and cook for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve with egg noodles or mashed potatoes.
