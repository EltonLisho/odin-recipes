Beef Goulash

Image by Freepik

Description

A classic Eastern European recipe, prepared with tender chunks of beef, savory vegetables, and fragrant spices. It is a family favorite comfort food, and absolutely delicious when served with egg noodles or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 pounds beef chuck roast
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • flour
  • Olive oil
  • unsalted butter
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • paprika
  • ground carraway seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef stock broth
  • egg noodles or mashed potatoes to serve alongside
